
### Territories

The **_Kingdom of Llwyrnau_** occupies a portion of the North-Western section of the [Isle of Corin](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/isle-of-corin-article) consisting of four major settlements, including the capital city of [Gwelendwyr](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/gwelendwyr-article). The majority of the territory consists of the [Merynau Forest](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/merynau-forest-article). The Forbidden Land to the North of the [Isle of Corin](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/isle-of-corin-article) are also under the protection of the Wood-Elves, anyone who should try to enter these lands without permission from the [[Wood-Elf Royals]], or those who should voyage too close, shall suffer the consequences decided by the current ruler acting in advisory of The Sacred Grove of the Great Oak.

**Legislative Body**
The current ruling royals decide which laws are put into effect, upon council from The Sacred Grove of the Great Oak and may even write laws themselves.

**Judicial Body**
The noble families are responsible for ensuring these laws are carried out, along with the aid of guards, and the army.
