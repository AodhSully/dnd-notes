### Territories

The **_Kingdom of Tiirynur_** occupies a portion of the North-Western section of the [Isle of Corin](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/isle-of-corin-article) consisting of four settlements, including the capital city of [Valar](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/valar-article) .

**Capital**

[Valar](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/valar-article)

[[Tiiryn]]

**Government System**

Monarchy, Absolute

**Power Structure**

Feudal state

**Legislative Body**

The current ruling royals decide which laws are put into effect, and may even write laws themselves.

**Judicial Body**

The noble families are responsible for ensuring these laws are carried out, along with the aid of guards, and the army.
