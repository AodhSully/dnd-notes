## Social

### Family Ties

Born to the noble family of _DeVoss_ to _Duke Edwin DeVoss_ in **Beldor**. Jantle was sent to the [Temple of Divinities](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/temple-of-divinities-landmark) as a young child, where he was raised by _the High Priestess._ Later in life, having no children of his own, he decided to adopt [Lily Gisbey](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/lily-gisbey-person) in order to rescue her from being sent to an orphanage in [Thraldur](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/nifaalar-arborisyl/a/thraldur-article).
